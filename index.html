Biography

A four piece experimental/trip-rock band from Melbourne's underground scene, writing music which draws on their love of vintage tone and the avant-garde. Vocal melodies, intricate rhythms and an air of timelessness, Kalacoma move from intimacy to intensity, fusing electronic and acoustic elements seamlessly in their live performance. Kalacoma engineer and mix all their own recordings, creating a truly unique production style that plays as much a role in determining the sound as the instruments themselves.

The band

Nick Herrera Vocals/Guitar/Percussion/Producer, Alan Erpi Keys/Vocals/Samples/Percussion, Marin Sekesan Guitar/Effects/Percussion, & Benjamin Tansey Bass/Percussion
