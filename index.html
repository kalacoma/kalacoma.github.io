Biography

Alt-electronica 5-piece, fusing intricate rhythms and filmic soundscapes with a melodic and experimental edge. Drawing from diverse influences, creating a sound that is hard to pin down, yet seamless in its execution.

The band

Nick Herrera Vocals/Guitar/Percussion/Producer, Alan Erpi Keys/Vocals/Samples/Percussion, Marin Sekesan Guitar/Effects, Benjamin Tansey Bass/Percussion & Brendan Lees Drums/Percussion
